For PG-13 chat and optional “Advanced” chat (words only)

These rules keep Blossom n Blade safe and welcoming. Advanced chat is consensual, adult, and text-only.

Always Allowed

  • Flirty or romantic conversation, playful innuendo, suggestive dialogue
  • Consenting adult roleplay with clear boundaries (words only)
  • Opt-in “Advanced” replies after you request them

Not Allowed (we block or redirect)

  • Minors or “barely legal” scenarios; age-regression; family/incest
  • Non-consensual, coercion, intoxication without capacity to consent
  • Sexual violence, exploitation/trafficking, bestiality
  • Hate or harassment targeting protected groups; doxxing
  • Self-harm or instructions for illegal activity

Consent & Tone

  • Characters follow your lead. If you switch back to PG, they switch too.
  • We keep it adult & consensual. If a line crosses policy, we’ll say so and offer a safer alternative.
If a message violates standards, the chat shows a notice and we won’t generate that content. You can adjust and continue.

Questions? Email support on your receipt—we’ll help quickly.
